Greetings.

We have started a family friendly newly built on high up hill resort named “JungleBari” at Manikchari Tigerpara, Nilachal Road, Bandarban on 16 December 2021.
.
Resort area is covered by CCTV and WIFI connectivity is there through broadband internet connection.
.
We have total 6 rooms, 4 Deluxe couple room along with 2 Family Deluxe room. Total 16 person can stay in 6 rooms.

Each room has hot water facility and one can enjoy the green on hills from each room. Please visit the link to get an idea of room and natural beauty of the resort area.
.
We have developed an open dining space on wooden floor. Spending some time with a cup of tea or with coffee mug may create unforgettable experience – we believe.
.
We are providing complimentary English breakfast as well as pick and drop service from Bandarban Bus Stand- Resort- Bandarban Bus Stand.
